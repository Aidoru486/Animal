White Donkey

Did you know that the white donkey is at risk of extinction? There are about 90 individuals on the island of Asinara and in the state forest of Porto Conte (Sardinia).

  • Scientific Name: Equus asinus var. albina
  • Average Height: 90 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 25-30 years
  • Habitat: Island of Asinara and forest of Porto Conte (Sardinia)

The white donkey has a height of about 90 cm (a form of insular nanism), heavy head and short neck, thin and robust limbs. The skin is rosy while the iris is pale blue. The albinism is responsible for photophobia.
